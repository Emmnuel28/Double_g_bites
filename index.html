What is Chin Chin?

Discover this delicious West African snack

A Taste of West Africa

Chin chin is a popular West African fried pastry snack similar to doughnuts or small biscuits. This crunchy, sweet delicacy is made from flour, sugar, butter, and sometimes milk or eggs.

At Double G Bites, we prepare our chin chin using traditional Nigerian recipes passed down through generations. Each batch is handcrafted with love and the finest ingredients to ensure an authentic taste that brings back memories of home.
